### ClassInSight
**Jan 2018 – Jun 2021**  
<a class="external-link" href="https://www.hcii.cmu.edu/news/2018/25m-grant-received-study-science-teaching">ClassInSight</a> is a 5-year design-based research project funded by the James S.
